He waited at the counter. The clerk did not look up. There was a form on the clipboard. He filled in his name. He filled in the date. The pen was chained to the desk. The chain was short. Outside a bus stopped and did not open its doors. A woman held a bag of oranges. One orange fell. It rolled under the bench. She did not pick it up. The clerk stamped the form. He stamped it twice. Next window, he said. There was no next window. There was a door. The door was locked. He sat down on the bench. The orange was still there. The bus left. He left it.
